About

  • ITF

    Intelligent Transformation Finance (ITF) an engineering focused team within Microsoft's Finance organization in Commerce Finance and Intelligence. ITF creates a modern finance roadmap, leveraging Microsoft’s own technologies, to modernize key financial processes and tools in partnership with the finance organization globally.

  • Mission

    Our mission is to empower Corporates Finance and Services (CFS) to lead the modernization of finance by creating innovative solutions and accelerating business operational efficiencies. We specialize in intelligent reporting, modern automation, digital collaboration, and innovation. 

  • Vision

    We are committed to improving our processes to be more efficient, cost-effective, and faster using OpenAI service.

  • Goals

    Our main goal is to create Enterprise ChatGPT solutions that use Finance data and tools like Bot SDK, Composer  Semantic Kernel (SK), TPrompt, … and help other Finance teams adopt this technology for their specific needs.
